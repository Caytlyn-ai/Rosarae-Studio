About Rosarae Studio

Handmade blooms, thoughtful details, and bouquets designed to last far beyond the moment they are given.

Rosarae Studio began with a simple love for handmade gifts that feel deeply personal. Over time, that love turned into a creative business centered around ribbon rose bouquets that never wilt and never lose their meaning.

Each bouquet is carefully folded by hand using satin ribbon, shaped petal by petal, and arranged with intention. I pay close attention to color, texture, wrapping, and every finishing detail so each piece feels special from the first glance.

Whether you are celebrating a birthday, an anniversary, Mother's Day, a wedding, or simply want something beautiful to keep, I want every Rosarae Studio piece to feel like it was made just for you.

Custom orders are always welcome, and larger event orders can be planned one detail at a time. No two bouquets are exactly alike, and that is part of what makes each arrangement meaningful.

- Rosarae Studio

What Matters Most

Lasting beauty. Thoughtful craftsmanship. A gift that feels personal from start to finish. That is what guides every bouquet I make.

If you have a vision in mind, I would love to help bring it to life with custom colors, specialty ribbon, add-ons, and finishing details that make it truly yours.
