About Me

Add your photo

Hi, I'm the heart behind Rosarae Studio. What started as a deep love for handmade things grew into something I'm truly passionate about — creating ribbon rose bouquets that are built to last long after fresh flowers fade.

Every single bouquet I make is hand-folded using premium satin ribbon. I pay attention to every detail, every color choice, every petal — because I believe the things we give to the people we love should feel as special as they are.

Whether you're celebrating a birthday, a wedding, a mother, or just want to treat yourself to something beautiful — I'm here to make it happen. Custom orders are always open, and no two bouquets are exactly the same.

— Rosarae Studio
