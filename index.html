About Rosarae Studio

Hi! I'm the heart behind Rosarae Studio. What started as a love for all things handmade grew into a passion for creating ribbon rose bouquets that truly last — no wilting, no waiting, just beauty that stays.

Every bouquet I make is hand-folded from premium satin ribbon with care and attention to every petal. Whether you're celebrating someone special or treating yourself, I'm here to make it beautiful.

I believe every order deserves a personal touch, which is why custom orders are always open. No two bouquets are exactly alike — and that's exactly how I like it.
